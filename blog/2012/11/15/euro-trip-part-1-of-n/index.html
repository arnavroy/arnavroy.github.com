Euro Trip: Part 1 of N

Nov 15th, 2012 | Comments

This post is a brain dump about my short trip to Europe in the month of May’12. I have tried to jot down whatever I remember in a tl;dr fashion.

  1. Zurich

    • Apps/Sites/Cards: Swiss Rail Android App, Swiss Flexi Pass
    • Hostel: Backpackers Villa Sonnenhof at Interlaken
    • Things I tried/Places I visited:
      • Laederach: Home made Swiss chocolates.
      • Lindt Factory: To get the feel of Willy Wonka and the Chocolate Factory.
      • Gornergrat: The highest open-air railway track in Europe.
      • Schilthorn: The panoramic revolving restaurant named Piz Gloria, where the movie “James Bond: On Her Majesty’s Secret Service” was shot.
      • Interlaken: A picturesque town located between two Alpine lakes.
      • Zermatt: Majestic view of Klein Matterhorn, the entire town is a car free zone.
      • Bern: The capital city has a unique combination of old and new architecture.
      • Lucerne: Visit the Chapel Bridge, oldest woodbridge in Europe. Lucerne Art Museum known for it’s modern art and architecture.
      • Lugano: Italian speaking Swiss town known for the mountain Monte San Salvatore.
      • Uetliberg: Panoramic view of Zurich City.
      • Cheese, wine and liquor chocolates.

  2. Munich

    • Apps/Sites/Cards: German Rail Services, Munich City Tour Card
    • Hostel: Euro Youth Hostel
    • Things I tried/Places I visited:
      • English Garden: A really huge park with musicians, slack-liners, frisbee players and a lot more.
      • Radler beer at Augustiner Keller: The best beer I’ve ever had.
      • BMW Museum
      • Olympic Stadium: A unique tent like architecture.
      • Statue of Bavaria: A witness to Octoberfest since 1850.
      • Nymphenburg Palace: Huge water bodies, birds everywhere and brilliant architecture.
      • Pretzel
      • Beer Gardens

  3. Prague

    • Apps/Sites/Cards: Short term ticket
    • Hostel: Old Prague Hostel
    • Things I tried/Places I visited:
      • Prague Castle: The biggest ancient castle in the world.
      • St. Vitus. Cathedral: Gothic architecture. Bohemian kings’ tombs.
      • Clock Tower: The oldest working astronomical clock in the world.
      • Cheap beer.

    • Pics

      Old Prague

  4. Amsterdam

    • Apps/Sites/Cards: Netherlands Rail Services, iAmsterdam Card
    • Hostel: Hostel Van Gogh
    • Things I tried/Places I visited:
      • Van Gogh Museum
      • Diamond Museum
      • De Wallen: The world famous red light district.
      • Cheese Museum: Try Gouda, Edam and Truffle cheese.
      • Canal Rides
      • Koog Zandaag: The wind mill town.
      • Leidseplein: The town square always bustles with performance artists, tourists and natives.

Thanks to the excellent public transportation system, friendly people, Wikitravel, Google Maps, Lonely Planet and Triposo Travel Guide; the trip went very smoothly. And thank you Raman for taking these nice pictures.

I hope you find this post to be useful in planning a euro trip.
